[2.0.0] - 2025.02.18

**Added**

**Changed**
-	Most of the textures for the palm wood set, furniture and decorative elements have been changed to have a less modern style.
-	The Beach Chair has been renamed the Hooded Beach Chair. It now has two parts, a bottom and a top, and can be connected.
-	The palm saplings have been changed to palm sprouts. Unlike other saplings, they do not drop from leaves. You can get them with a small chance by opening coconuts.

**Fixed**



**Note:**
With 2.0.0 Beachparty got slightly overhauled and reworked. Texture Changes, Naming Conventions and new or reworked features.

_Why this rework?_ <br>
Beachparty currently lacks a cohesive theme and structured feature design. Instead, it feels like a collection of loosely beach-related content without a clear direction. While the existing elements do capture a beach vibe, they don't form a engaging experience.
With this rework, we aim to give Beachparty a more personal touch and introduce unique, well-thought-out features that enhance the overall experience. Not every addition needs to impact gameplay directly - some features can simply be for fun and aesthetics, such as sandcastles or beach goals. However, even decorative elements should offer a small functional purpose, making them more meaningful in the game world.
The goal is to create a cohesive, engaging, and enjoyable beach-themed experience, where every feature feels intentional rather than just another random addition.
